Controversial Strategy Against Climate Change Biodiversity | Natural Capital | Sustainability
Nobody doubts that deforestation is one of the factors that exacerbate climate change, even more so when these lands previously occupied by forests are converted to agricultural, industrial or urban use. And these changes are often irreversible; it is not possible to recover the lost forest cover at the stroke of a pen, nor can we do without these homes, factories or food production operations.
